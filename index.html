Shadow over Ragusa

The first part of an epic historical saga where pure love blooms amid the shadows of war

Synopsis

Centuries ago on the Adriatic, the Republic of Ragusa survives through diplomacy, trade, and the vigilance of its walls. As Venetian pressure grows from the west, a quieter danger begins to move within the city itself. What begins as a troubling discovery soon unravels into a deeper conspiracy, reaching from monastery corridors to the very heart of the council chamber. Ragusa’s political life moves through ritual and ceremony, yet beneath the surface, loyalties begin to fracture and hidden forces begin to act. Shadow over Ragusa blends mystery, romance, and political intrigue into the story of a small republic standing between empires.
