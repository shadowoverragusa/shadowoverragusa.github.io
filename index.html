Shadow over Ragusa

A historical novel set in the Republic of Ragusa, where diplomacy, loyalty, and love endure under pressure from empires.

Synopsis

Centuries ago on the Adriatic, the Republic of Ragusa survives by wit, trade, and restraint. When a hidden crime on Lokrum Island reveals foreign intrigue, a household steward and a city official uncover a conspiracy that threatens the city’s fragile independence.
